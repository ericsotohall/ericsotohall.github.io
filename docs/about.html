About Me

Hello! My name is Eric Soto-Hall and I have always loved things with wheels. From trains to bicycles, to cars, to motorcycles — that fascination eventually expanded into the transportation industry as a whole.

I recently earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering in December 2025. While my formal education provided strong theoretical foundations, I have learned just as much through hands-on mechanical and electrical projects.

This website exists to document those projects and the lessons behind them. I am deeply grateful to everyone who made these experiences possible — and to any engineers, technicians, or recruiters who take the time to explore my work.
