Complete frame-up restoration of a 1995 Honda XR600 dual-sport motorcycle. Project involved full teardown to the bare frame, mechanical refurbishment, suspension overhaul, and a ground-up electrical rebuild. The factory wiring harness was removed and replaced with a custom-built harness, including new routing, terminations, grounding strategy, and component integration. Emphasis was placed on reliability, serviceability, and correct electrical practices suitable for off-road vibration and environmental exposure.
